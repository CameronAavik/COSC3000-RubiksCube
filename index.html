To use the Rubik's Cube:

"x" rotates about the x axis (x axis points right)
"y" rotates about the y axis (y axis points up)
"z" rotates about the z axis (z axis points to you)

"f" rotates the white centered face
"b" rotates the yellow centered face
"l" rotates the blue centered face
"r" rotates the green centered face
"u" rotates the red centered face
"d" rotates the orange centered face

To perform the anti-clockwise version of these, hold shift while pressing the button
